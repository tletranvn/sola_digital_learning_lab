### Freedom begins with a distinction

Epictetus asks us to separate our **judgment and response** from outcomes that no effort can fully
guarantee. This is not an invitation to withdraw. It is a way to act without treating uncertainty as
if it were already under our command.

<KeyIdea>
  Control describes our present judgment and choice. Influence describes action whose result remains
  partly open.
</KeyIdea>

<Callout title="A contemporary example">
  After an interview, preparation and conduct belong to your action. The committee’s final decision
  does not.
</Callout>
